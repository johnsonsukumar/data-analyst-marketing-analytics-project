# 📊 Business Problem Overview — Marketing Analytics Project

## 🏢 Business Context

ShopEasy, an e-commerce retail company, is experiencing a decline in customer engagement and conversion rates despite increased investment in marketing campaigns. This has resulted in lower return on investment (ROI) and reduced overall marketing effectiveness.

The company collects data from multiple sources, including website interactions, campaign performance metrics, and customer feedback (reviews and social media). However, these data points are not being effectively utilized to drive business decisions.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Despite higher marketing spend, ShopEasy is facing:

* Decreasing customer engagement across digital channels
* Declining conversion rates from visitors to customers
* Inefficient marketing campaigns with poor ROI
* Lack of actionable insights from customer feedback

The business needs a data-driven approach to identify performance gaps and optimize marketing strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this analysis are:

* Increase conversion rates by identifying drop-off points in the customer journey
* Improve customer engagement by analyzing content performance
* Enhance customer satisfaction through feedback and sentiment analysis
* Optimize marketing spend by identifying high-performing campaigns

---

## 📌 Key Business Questions

This project aims to answer the following:

* Which factors are driving low conversion rates?
* Which marketing channels and content types generate the highest engagement?
* What patterns exist in customer behavior across demographics and time?
* What are the common themes in customer feedback (positive & negative)?
* How can marketing strategies be improved to maximize ROI?

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The success of this project is measured using:

* **Conversion Rate** — % of visitors who make a purchase
* **Customer Engagement Rate** — clicks, likes, interactions
* **Average Order Value (AOV)** — revenue per transaction
* **Customer Feedback Score** — average rating from reviews

---

## 🧠 Analytical Approach

To address the business problem, the following approach is used:

* Data extraction and cleaning using SQL & Python
* Exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends and patterns
* Sentiment analysis on customer feedback
* KPI development and tracking using Power BI
* Dashboard creation for stakeholder decision-making

---

## 🚀 Expected Outcomes

* Identification of key drivers behind low engagement and conversion
* Insights into customer preferences and behavior patterns
* Data-driven recommendations for marketing optimization
* Improved customer experience and satisfaction
* Increased marketing ROI and business performance

---

## 👥 Stakeholders

* Marketing Team
* Customer Experience Team
* Business Leadership
* Data & Analytics Team

---

## 💡 Business Impact

This project enables ShopEasy to transition from intuition-based decisions to a **data-driven marketing strategy**, improving campaign effectiveness, customer engagement, and overall revenue growth.
